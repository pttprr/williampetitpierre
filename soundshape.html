sound.shape

musical instrument

role: audio programmer

listen

the sound.shape project began with the desire to control electronic sound in a tangible manner.

this naturally led to the conception of a sensor made of connected textiles, translating hand position data into sound.

sensel's morph controller replaced the handmade sensor, drastically increasing data accuracy and variety.

finger positions are mapped to numerous sound parameters through machine learning, allowing the sound to evolve accordingly.
