sound delta

immersive audio device

role: audio programmer

latest project: cargo

sound delta is a device dedicated to crafting immersive soundscapes exploring a new form of composition where sound and space merge.

equipped with an audio headset and a terminal, the observer embarks on an odyssey through spaces ranging from the intimate scale of a square, to the dimensions of an entire city.

the trip leads to the contemplation of a vast soundscape amidst several listening points.

the convergence of sound and space gives birth to a succession of fleeting and intangible auditory climates, engendering an augmented reality imbued with poetic resonance.
